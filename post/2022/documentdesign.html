Document Design

2022–09–25 ~2000 words

As the title suggests, the following is about interacting with different types of text based formats and how one should prepare documents so they can fulfill their functions. This is the follow-up to the Information Management post.

Once a piece of nonfiction is written based on collected information, one should give due consideration to copyediting with style guidelines like “The Chicago Manual of Style” by the University of Chicago Press[1], “The Elements of Style” by William Strunk Jr.[2] and “The Economist Style Guide” by Ann Wroe[3] for consistent quality in writing.

Choice of Format

A document refers to an object, which stores information that serve as a record. Accessability of information is thereby the primary function of documentation. Different means of access require different modes of presentation. Starting from the most adaptable format, plain text, based on the intended function of the document, one can choose a fitting format for proceeding with refining the type and layout. A good combination of markup languages is LaTeX for PDFs and html+css for WWW or EPUB, both complementarily compensating each others shortcomings, henceforward I would like to focus my deliberations on those.

The plain text document base still requires a minimum of markup, with markdown and org-mode1 being the most prominent lightweight markup languages. At this stage, the document is not for public sharing, but certain standards like proper citations should be considered to serve as a useful source later on. A good fit is the pandoc document converter software, with extensible conversion operations to LaTeX and html+css from its pandoc flavored markdown specification. Markdown documents can be initialized with a YAML metadata block to denote the author, date and title. By using the extended pandoc markdown syntax, sources can be cited from a biblatex file with [@CiteKey] and a .csl citation style file; further footnotes may be added by [^Key], immensely reducing the amount of adjustments and typesetting required to produce reasonable results for both LaTeX and html based formats from a single text. The raw_attribute extension allows conversion format conditional text blocks. The pandoc output can be improved with custom regular expression substitutions pre and post conversion, for example in python with a the subprocess and re standard libraries. Though compromises have to be made by adhering to this smallest common denominator of a simple markdown syntax, it ensures a quicker workflow and is a good starting point for more refined typesetting later on.

Most documents will benefit from including vector graphics and diagrams as pictorial, yet abstract, representations of information. Graph drawing tools include inkscape, graphviz, diagrams.net and PGF/TikZ.

PDF Template

Template version 2023–02–26 source— ZIP

For PDF generation, LaTeX and its document classes enable the design of many types of documents from books, presentations and articles, to letters, flashcards or curriculum vitae.2

The mainly used template of mine can adapt to different design requirements, by alteration of the document structure based on assigned boolean inputs for conditional statements in the main document.

\documentclass[]{this_class}
\newcommand\true{1}

%------------------------------------------------
%   Document layout (0=false 1=true)
%------------------------------------------------
\newcommand\conditionA{0}

\usepackage{style} 

%------------------------------------------------
%   Document structure
%------------------------------------------------
\begin{document}

\ifx\conditionA\true
    \input{contentA}
\else
    \input{contentB}
\fi

\end{document}

A good document viewer for the output PDF is SumatraPDF, because it allows you to change the background and text color for reading in low light conditions.

The LaTeX template has multiple cover options. Lightweight options are maketitle for short documents, the titlepage environment, or inputting an external background and writing on top of it with the eso-pic and textpos packages.

A simple titlepage is the best option for most extensive documents, but if you want to make a bound print copy of your text, you are best served by a proper book cover including a spine. LaTeX might not be the best option for most styles of cover artwork and one would be better served with a vector graphics editor like inkscape, but covers reminiscent of the New Typography movement could certainly be created without too much of a hassle.3 It becomes clear why that is, if we consider the history of typography.

The 15th century saw the invention of the printing press, imitating the medieval handwritten manuscript design and black letter typeface. Around the turn of the century in Italy, antiqua typefaces (roman and italic type) where developed with printing in mind, increasing the bodytext’s fluency. Italian Renaissance culture spread throughout Europe by impetus of the Italian wars from 1494 to 1559 and saw innovations by the French, like Garamond. The popularity of antiqua initiated the subsequent design of Fraktur in Germany, a mellowed but as the name suggests, still distinctly fractured black letter typeface. The cultural influence of the French Rococo, sealed the transition towards French antiqua as the dominant class of typefaces across most of Europe. Clear and simple forms and the contrast in stroke thickness of French antiqua types prompt a logical progression in step with the themes of Enlightenment in the 17th and 18th centuries. Modern roman typefaces from Didot employ mathematical precision and geometric shapes. The Industrial Revolution in the 19th century spawned new production processes. The need for flashy, “grotesque” display and advertisement fonts along with trends in modern art, makes sans serif a popular alternative to antiqua in a broadening array of use cases. 1920s’ New Typography movement is a reactive response to late 19th century Art nouveau inspired printing, considered retrogressive due to its reintroduction of ornaments, by modernist typographers like Jan Tschichold, Herbert Bayer and László Moholy-Nagy.

“The essence of the New Typography is clarity. This puts it into deliberate opposition to the old typography whose aim was”beauty” and whose clarity did not attain the high level we require today. ” — The New Typography by Jan Tschichold 1928, p.66 4

I consider TeX to be a consistent progression of this mentality, but focusing less on merely exposing a printed pieces essential function, and more on providing the designer with a toolset to convey their message, allowing for a sliver of artistic expression. Even Tschichold himself repudiated his books intolerant and strict claim to absoluteness after reconsidering his cultural bias in light of World War II.

When the document is about to be printed, the printing company will provide the required cover measurements based on their production process and the selected paper thickness. Consider that the inner margin of long documents may have to be increased due to folding. The spine should follow ISO-6357 according to which the text should run from top to bottom, when the book is upright.

This design mainly utilizes the bookcover, xcoffins and tikz packages and imitates, in parts, the 1925 issue “sonderheft elementare typografie” of the magazine “typografische mitteilungen”, based on an exemplary implementation.5

Webpage Template

The html+css style for the World Wide Web should be somewhat congruent with the LaTeX template. The static site generator takes pandoc’s output markdown files, executes the conversion to html and applies the template accordingly. As html+css is not agnostic to the document viewer’s dimensions, insisting on ideal replication is both unfeasible and unnecessary. It’s adaptability requires new guidelines; for example, whereas in PDF’s there is no need to strip images of their metadata individually, images included in html documents should be examined with exiftool prior to publication. I find that justified text does not look pleasing on websites, the interword spacing is often distracting. As this website is build by the static site generator jekyll utilizing the minima theme, most of the publication design is determined by modifications to the predefined settings.[8] After quite a few iterations, I now gladly consider this publication design congruent with its intended function. Notably, the orthographic best practices are independent of format and are applicable to all documents. When in doubt, my reference of choice is the “Chicago Manual of Style” since even the best template requires copyediting.

As German law demands legal information such as legal notices and data privacy policies to be constantly and easily accessible from every subpage, integrating them in the global website footer is most convenient. In the _includes folder, add contact.html in Liquid syntax to the theme’s footer.html and create those files. In contact.html just add assets/pages/contact.md in Liquid syntax, and create that path. With permalink: /contact/ in the document yaml-head, the legal notice will be build and linked correctly.

I added custom sub- and superscript css properties to eliminate line spacing irregularities caused by endnotes.

//custom: eliminate line spacing irregularities
sup {
  vertical-align: super; 
  line-height: 1ex;
}

sub {
  vertical-align: sub;
  line-height: 1ex;
}

I also added a use_math: true/false YAML field, only loading mathjax when needed. Mathjax’s CSS container mjx-container should be changed to overflow-x: auto; for display math and overflow-x: hidden; for inline math mjx-container:not([display]), else the layout breaks with long equations and unneeded scrollbars.

EPUB Template

For comfortable offline reading on screens, a pandoc conversion to EPUB is a good option. EPUB is an open source, XML based format that supports both fixed and reflowable layout formatting and a subset of MathML; in effect, such a file is an archive that contains a website. For most types of electronic publications, EPUB should be superior to PDF since the content can be processed and extracted, for example with regular expressions.
